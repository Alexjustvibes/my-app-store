# Concept: Resonance (and perspective, its precondition)

Resonance is what happens when a piece of work carries a frequency only *you* could have generated, and it reaches the receiver it was meant for. It moves people through **recognition**, not argument.

## The human's working version
From [[sources/ai-reenchantment-alex-2026-03-01]]:
- **Resonance vs. persuasion:** "Persuasion moves people through argument. Resonance moves them through recognition." In contact with the work, the receiver feels something true has been named — something they knew but had no words for.
- **You can't manufacture it.** You can only "develop the perspective deep enough to produce it."
- **Perspective ≠ opinion.** Opinion is *what* you think; perspective is the *altitude* you see from — breadth of view, depth of pattern recognition, range of analogies. (Likely overlaps his "Levels of Awareness" framework — worth linking when that page exists.)
- **The operational formula:** "Judgment without creativity is execution. Creativity without judgment is noise." Perspective developed deep enough to produce both = "resonance made operational."
- **Convergent vs. singular solutions:** a problem solved without creativity is the *average* solution — correct, but it "was always going to exist; you were not required for it." A creative solution is singular because it needed a specific perspective to find. Quality comes from inaccessibility.
