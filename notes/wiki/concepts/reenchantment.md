# Concept: Reenchantment

Re-investing your *own* life and work with meaning, mythology, and weight — deliberately, from the inside — rather than waiting to discover meaning "out there." The "re" is the point: not first enchantment, but the active recovery of it in a disenchanted, productivity-flattened world.

## The human's working version
From [[sources/ai-reenchantment-alex-2026-03-01]]:
- Meaning isn't found externally; it's *generated* — "re-enchant your own life, specifically, with your own mythologies, your own ideas." Artifacts carry weight "because they were built by you and for you."
- It's an *orientation*, not a technology: the same AI is a productivity tool or a "reenchantment engine" depending on whether you bring it tasks or **hungers**.
- The mechanism is excavation, not invention: "the loop does not invent, it excavates" — pressing on what's already in you until the shape becomes visible.
