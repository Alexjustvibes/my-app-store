# Concept: Alignment (the highest form of freedom)

The human's own claim: **alignment is the highest form of freedom — above time-freedom and financial-freedom — but you may need both of those to unlock it.** Alignment = your daily actions, the way you earn, and who you are all pointing the same direction.

## The human's working version
From [[sources/own-notes-batch-pre-wiki]] (his own prior notes):
- "Alignment is the highest form of freedom… better than time-freedom and better than financial-freedom, but you may need both in order to unlock it." (note 3)
- Applied to business: "It isn't about making money — you can make money doing anything. It's about *how* you make money, what levers you're pulling… you'll spend the majority of your time pulling the money levers, so find ones you enjoy pulling." (note 4)

The two notes are one idea at two altitudes: alignment as a *life* state (note 3) and alignment as a *business-model* choice (note 4 — pick the levers, not just the payout).

## How it sits in the wiki
- It's the telos the rest of the economic spine was missing. Naval's [[concepts/leverage]] / [[concepts/wealth-vs-status]] tell you how to get free *time and money*; alignment says those are means, not the end.
- It's the positive pole of [[concepts/agency]] (escaping tax-slave/mind-slave) and the steady-state of [[concepts/flow]] — the jester-hero "goes with the flow of reality and it rewards him" ([[frameworks/the-jester-hero]]) is alignment by another name.
- "Find levers you enjoy pulling" = [[concepts/specific-knowledge]] applied to a business model (the work that feels like play).
