This is the classic engagement-vs-alignment trap (ironically, the thing [[concepts/alignment]] warns about). Options recorded for the human's call:
- Treat it as **top-of-funnel** bait that earns attention you then redirect to on-thesis work.
- **Reframe it onto the goal** via [[concepts/compounding]]: 200k years and no one compounded anything for you → owned creative assets are how you become the first link who leaves something. That bridge makes it serve the bet.
- Or accept it as off-strategy and not chase the dragon.
