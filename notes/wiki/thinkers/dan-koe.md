# Thinker: Dan Koe

The creator-economy writer/educator behind the "one-person business" and "2 Hour Writer / 2 Hour Content" material. A core recurring mind for this wiki — his process models map closely onto the human's own frameworks, which makes him both a source of scaffolding and a sparring partner.

## Core claims (as they appear so far)
- Writing is a **balance of creativity and productivity**, run as two distinct modes ([[concepts/two-modes-of-attention]]). See [[sources/dan-koe-creativity-vs-productivity]].
- Most "writing" is upstream of typing — it happens in idea generation and outlining.
- **Brainstorm, then let it simmer**: rest activates the subconscious, which does the connective work. Outline a week ahead; write spaced out.
- "Good writing cannot be forced." Creativity = connection; productivity = clarity.
- Business has a spiritual / "do nothing" dimension (otium by another name — [[concepts/otium-negotium]]).
- References a **2 Hour Content ecosystem** as the practical system this theory feeds (referenced, not yet detailed in ingested material).
- "Entrepreneurship is self-improvement in disguise." (from [[sources/commonplace-2026-06-09]]) — the one-person business as a personal-development vehicle, not just an income one. _(The human later reposted this line as his own note — [[sources/own-notes-batch-pre-wiki]] — so it's a belief he's adopted, not just recorded.)_

## Where I diverge
